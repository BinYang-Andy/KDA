K/DA Members Profile

K/DA is a virtual K-Pop girl group under Riot Games and are also in the game created by Riot Games known as League of Legends. The group consists of Ahri, Evelynn, Kai’sa, and Akali. The group debuted on November 3rd, 2018 with POP/STAR.
